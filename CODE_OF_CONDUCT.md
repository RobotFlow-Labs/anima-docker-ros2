# Code of Conduct

RobotFlowLabs ANIMA is a public project. Everyone who participates is expected to keep the space usable for other people.

This project follows a simple standard:

- be respectful
- be direct
- assume good intent
- criticize ideas and code, not people
- keep public collaboration constructive

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, doxxing, threats, and persistent bad-faith disruption.

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that damage the working environment.

For serious concerns, contact RobotFlowLabs maintainers privately.
